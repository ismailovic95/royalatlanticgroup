ELEX WooCommerce Product Price Custom Text (Before & After Text) and Discount simplifies the task to add a text before and after the product price both globally and individually. It also allows you to apply a quick discount for your products.

== Description ==

ELEX WooCommerce Product Price Custom Text (Before & After Text) and Discount plugin helps you to quickly add a before and after text for the WooCommerce product prices. The plugin even allows you to quickly apply a percentage & flat discount to your WooCommerce products. Both discounts for WooCommerce products and the custom text for the product price can be applied globally and even for the individual products.

= Features =
* Easily set up before & after text for your WooCommerce Product Prices displayed for your Customers.
* Apply percentage & flat discount on your WooCommerce Products Prices.
* Customisation options for Product Price Custom Text & Discounts.
* Supports WooCommerce Simple and Variable Products .

= Few of the Use Cases =
* Apply discount for WooCommerce Product on a percentage & flat rate basis.
* Apply custom prefix and suffix text with product price across all of the WooCommerce products listed on your eCommerce store.
* Apply custom prefix and suffix text with product price for only the specific WooCommerce products listed on your eCommerce store.

1. Upload the plugin folder to the ‘/wp-content/plugins/’ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins’ menu in WordPress.
3. That's it – you can now configure the plugin.
